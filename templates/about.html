About Me

Hi there, thank you for being here! My name is Tushar and I’m a Dubai-born, Ontario-based recording artist. I’ve lived in 4 different countries so far and have been on the move a lot; I was born in Dubai, and I lived there for the first 18 years of my life.

I then went to Mississippi where I studied economics with a focus in mathematics on an academic scholarship. After Mississippi, I moved to Dubai for a few years where I worked in finance. I’m now in Toronto.

Needless to say, my life has been atypical, and I’ve been a bit of a rolling stone. However, the one thing that has always remained constant is my art. I started my musical journey at the age of 8 and have never really stepped away.

Creating work of my own has been both my reprieve in times of stress, and my mood booster during times of joy. My music is the one domain where I can express so much of what I otherwise cannot.

Outside of music, I really enjoy running, swimming and hanging out with the people I love.
